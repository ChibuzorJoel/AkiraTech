We collect information you voluntarily provide when you register for an account, fill out contact or inquiry forms, apply for scholarships, enrol in courses, make payments, subscribe to newsletters, or communicate with us. 3. How We Use Your Information

To Provide Our Services: Process enrolment and applications, deliver educational content, manage accounts and profiles, process payments, provide customer support.

To Improve Our Services: Analyse platform usage, identify technical issues, improve features and functionality.

For Communications: Send important updates, notify users of policy changes, send newsletters and educational content.

For Legal and Compliance: Comply with Nigerian laws, prevent fraud and abuse, enforce our Terms of Service.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information with payment processors, email service providers, cloud hosting providers, analytics providers, and customer support tools. All service providers are required to handle your information securely.

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information, including SSL/TLS encryption, secure storage systems, access controls, security assessments and updates, and staff training on data protection.
