Our Story

From Lagos to the
World Wide Web

Akiira Tech was Founded with a singular mission — to make world-class digital solutions accessible to Nigerian businesses. We saw too many great ideas fail because of poor digital execution, and we decided to change that.

From our first website in 2019, we have grown into a full-service studio delivering websites, mobile apps, e-commerce platforms and digital marketing for clients across Nigeria and beyond.

Today we are proud to be the trusted partner of over 50 businesses — from solo founders to established brands — all united by a desire to grow online.
