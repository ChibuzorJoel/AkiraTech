03 Non-Refundable Items

The following are strictly non-refundable under all circumstances:

  • Upfront deposit / retainer fee (50% of project value)
  • Domain name registrations purchased on your behalf
  • Hosting fees and server setup costs
  • SSL certificates and third-party licensing fees
  • Rush fees for expedited delivery
  • Completed and approved design revisions
  • Consultation fees for discovery sessions
  • Third-party API or service subscriptions activated for your project
